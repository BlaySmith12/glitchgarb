Shipping Information

Everything you need to know about getting your GLITCHGARB order.

GHANA (LOCAL SHIPPING)

REGION DELIVERY TIME COST
Greater Accra 1-2 business days FREE
Other Regions 3-5 business days FREE
Remote Areas 5-7 business days GH₵ 20

INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING

REGION DELIVERY TIME COST
West Africa 5-10 business days GH₵ 50
Rest of Africa 7-14 business days GH₵ 80
Europe 10-14 business days GH₵ 120
North America 10-21 business days GH₵ 150
Rest of World 14-28 business days GH₵ 180

SHIPPING PARTNERS

We work with trusted delivery partners to ensure your order arrives safely:

  • Ghana Post - Local deliveries
  • DHL - International express
  • FedEx - International standard
  • Local courier services for Accra metro area

TRACKING YOUR ORDER

Once your order ships, you'll receive:

  • Email confirmation with tracking number
  • SMS notification (Ghana only)
  • Real-time tracking updates

You can also track your order in your account under Order History.

IMPORTANT NOTES

  • Delivery times are estimates and may vary during peak periods
  • Signature may be required upon delivery
  • We are not responsible for delays caused by customs or local postal services
  • Please ensure your shipping address is complete and accurate
