About Us

Defined by exclusivity. Built for the street.
Ghana's premier drop-based label.

GLITCHGARB was born from a vision to bring world-class streetwear to Africa, creating limited pieces that tell stories and build community.

OUR STORY

Founded in Accra, Ghana, GLITCHGARB emerged from the intersection of African creativity and global streetwear culture. We saw a gap in the market for premium, limited-edition streetwear that speaks to the African experience while resonating with a global audience.

Every piece we create is a statement. We don't follow trends—we set them. Our designs draw inspiration from the energy of African cities, the rhythm of the streets, and the spirit of a generation that refuses to be defined by boundaries.

We believe in quality over quantity. That's why we operate on a drop-based model, releasing limited quantities of each design. When you wear GLITCHGARB, you're wearing something exclusive—something that won't be mass-produced or found everywhere.

THE DROP MODEL

Unlike traditional fashion brands that produce season after season, we release limited "drops"—curated collections that are available for a limited time only. This approach allows us to:

  • Maintain the highest quality standards
  • Reduce waste and overproduction
  • Create genuine exclusivity for our customers
  • Stay responsive to our community's preferences
  • Keep our designs fresh and innovative
